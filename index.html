Trip Highlights

My trip to Fire Island was really fun. I saw the ocean, walked on the beach, and explored the sand dunes. I also saw animals and enjoyed the quiet environment away from the busy city. One of the best parts was seeing the lighthouse and watching the sunset over the water.
